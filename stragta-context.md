## What we do — Services

### 1. Technology Strategy
We audit your current technology stack, benchmark it against your sector, cut through
vendor noise, and deliver a clear roadmap. Your next technology move should be the right
one, not just the trending one.

Deliverables include: technology audit report, vendor comparison matrix, 12-month roadmap,
ROI projections.

Ideal for: companies unsure whether to invest in new tools, facing a major tech decision,
or getting oversold by vendors.

### 2. Digital Transformation
Technology without change management is just software collecting dust. We guide your
organisation through the human side of transformation: building internal buy-in,
redesigning processes around new tools, and making change stick long-term.

Deliverables include: process redesign documentation, change management plan, KPI framework,
team training materials.

Ideal for: organisations that have bought technology but are not fully using it, or are
planning a major internal process change.

### 3. Executive Training
Intensive, hands-on programs designed for decision-makers, not IT teams. Your leadership
leaves with real skills and a shared language for navigating technology — not just a
certificate they will never open again.

Programs run on-site or remotely. Duration: typically 1 to 5 days depending on scope.
Fully customised to your sector and team.

Topics covered: AI literacy for executives, technology evaluation frameworks, digital
strategy fundamentals, cybersecurity awareness for leadership, prompt engineering for
business use, AI tools for productivity.

Ideal for: executive teams, boards, senior managers who need to make technology decisions
without a deep technical background.

Example completed program: 4-day executive AI training at Instituto Garci, Madrid.

### 4. AI Integration
We map your operations, identify where artificial intelligence delivers measurable value,
and implement it properly. From generative AI workflows to intelligent automation — we
ensure your team actually adopts it, rather than licensing it and forgetting it.

Deliverables include: AI opportunity assessment, implementation plan, tool configuration,
team onboarding, 30-day follow-up check.

Technologies we work with: OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Meta Llama, Mistral, Google Gemini,
n8n, Make (Integromat), Zapier, custom API integrations, Stable Diffusion, ElevenLabs,
and others depending on your needs.

Ideal for: companies that want to use AI but do not know where to start, or have tried
tools that did not stick.

## Our approach — how an engagement works

Every engagement follows three steps:

**Step 1 — Diagnose**
We audit your current technology, workflows, and team readiness with no assumptions and
no pre-packaged answers. We identify where real opportunities and risks actually live
before recommending anything.

**Step 2 — Design**
We co-create a strategy with your team: technology choices, sequencing, success metrics.
Every recommendation is tied to a business outcome you already care about, not one we
invented to justify a deliverable.

**Step 3 — Deploy and Embed**
We implement alongside your team, train whoever needs it, and stay through the adoption
curve. The engagement ends when the technology is genuinely working — not when the
contract expires.

It depends on scope. A technology audit takes 2-3 weeks. A full transformation program
can run 3-6 months. Executive training programs run 1-5 days.

**Do you work with small businesses?**
Our typical client is a medium to large organisation, but we do work with ambitious small
businesses on a case-by-case basis.

**Can you help us choose between specific tools?**
Yes, vendor evaluation and comparison is a core part of our Technology Strategy service.

**Do you implement the technology yourselves or just advise?**
Both. We can advise only, or advise and implement, depending on your needs and internal
capacity.

**What makes you different from a large consulting firm?**

read reports. We actively use and test the technologies we recommend.

Use the contact form on the website. The first conversation is free and there is no
commitment. One of our team members will respond within one business day.
